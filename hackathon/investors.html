Frequently Asked Questions

What is your carbon verification (MRV) process?

+
We use a hybrid MRV (Monitoring, Reporting, Verification) model, combining satellite data with our proprietary app for on-ground data capture. All projects are audited by third-party validators under Verra or Gold Standard.

What are the investment models available?

+
We offer opportunities in equity investment (for scaling our platform), debt financing (for supply chain operations), and direct pre-purchase of carbon credits. Please contact our team for the full investor deck.

How do you ensure farmer retention and impact?

+
Our model is built on a "Farmer First" principle. By increasing their net income through premium pricing, carbon revenue, and lower input costs—backed by a buy-back guarantee—we ensure high retention and long-term, sustainable impact.
